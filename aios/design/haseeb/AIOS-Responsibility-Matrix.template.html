01Overview
How to Read This Matrix
This document splits every kind of problem into two simple groups. The rule is easy: we look after what we build. If something goes wrong because of the software we made, we fix it. If it comes from another company's service, from something you give us, or from normal running costs, it's outside what we look after, though we'll always help you find out what's wrong.

Group A — What We Look After

Anything that breaks because of the software, settings, or connections we set up: app bugs, one client seeing another's data, a connection we built failing, an automatic job going wrong, a report coming out wrong. If it starts on our side, we fix it.

Group B — Out of Our Hands

Things we can't control once the platform is running: an outside service going down, its price or rules changing, wrong data coming back from others, the keys and logins you provide, running costs, and the server you own after handover.

The main idea

We look after problems that come from the software we built, for the agreed support period. We can't be responsible for whether outside services stay online, what they charge, whether their data is right, or how they change their rules, nor for the keys you give us, nor for the day-to-day running costs. None of these are in our hands once the platform is live.

05In practice
How a Problem Gets Sorted Out
When something looks wrong, which group it falls into isn't a matter of opinion, it's decided by what actually caused it. Here's the exact path every reported problem follows, so there's never any question of who does what.
Step 1
Something looks wrong
You or the system notices something not working as it should.
Step 2
You tell us
It's reported to us with what happened, when, and which client.
Step 3
We look into it
We track down what actually caused it, using our records.
Step 4
We sort it into a group
The cause decides the group: our software, or an outside factor.

Group A · Our software

We fix it, at no extra cost, during the agreed support period. A bug, a data mix-up, a broken job, a wrong report, if it comes from what we built, it's on us and we sort it.

Group B · Outside factor

We tell you exactly what it is, a service outage, an expired key, a usage limit, a running cost, and advise how to fix it. If it needs paid work, we quote it first.

Either way, you're never in the dark

Every problem gets a clear answer, backed by our records, on what went wrong and why. How we sort it is open and based on the real cause, never on what's cheapest for us.
