Cost Is a Dial, Not a Fixed Bill
The most important thing to know about running AIOS: you decide what you spend. Free Google data does most of the everyday work. You only pay for extra data when a client is big enough to justify it. That is why a small client can run at almost nothing — and the whole setup stays cheap.

The only things you pay for: server + data

Server & Infrastructure

A fixed, steady base: your own private server, the database, backups and a web address. About $70–95/mo, no matter how many clients you have.

Live SEO Data

You pay only for what you use. Free Google data is always on; paid rank data from serper.dev runs at about ~$30/mo for all 15 clients.

Already Covered — $0

Google's free tools, the AI you already bought (Claude), and your in-house crawler all cost nothing extra here. They do the writing, analysis and audits.

The one lever that controls everything

Each client, and each feature, runs at one of three levels: manual (free), semi-automatic (low cost), or fully automatic (paid data). Turn a client up only when their budget pays for it — and turn it back down any time. Nothing is locked in.

And your keys stay locked away — at no extra cost

Every password and API key — yours, your clients', and Google's — is kept in an encrypted key vault (Supabase Vault) that comes free with the plan already listed. The key that unlocks it is stored separately, so even a stolen copy of the database can't be read.

Why it's pennies, not seats

One rank check costs about $0.001 (a tenth of a cent). Buying only the exact searches you need — instead of a fixed monthly seat — is what keeps all 15 clients near ~$30.

Adjusted per client

Every job above can be turned off or slowed (daily → weekly) per client. Small clients run mostly on free Google data; only premium clients get the full daily refresh.

serper.dev is prepaid, not a subscription — a $50 pack of 50,000 searches (valid 6 months) covers all 15 clients for roughly a month and a half. A full top-100 scan counts as 2 searches. Audits run on your in-house crawler ($0) and backlink checks come from Semrush/Ahrefs CSV exports ($0), so serper.dev is the only paid data line.
