A note on promises

This reference is intentionally conservative. Where a platform's behaviour, price, or link policy could not be confirmed, it is marked Unverified rather than assumed favourable. Automated posting and listing submission depend on third-party systems that change their APIs, pricing, CAPTCHA, and moderation without notice, and several explicitly restrict automation in their terms. Treat every "Yes" as "yes at time of writing, subject to the platform's current rules" — and confirm against the live source before quoting a specific outcome to a client.
