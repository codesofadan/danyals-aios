# Algorithm and update timeline - 2024 to 2026

Loaded by: A1 (Content), M3 (Critic).
Source: Google Search Status Dashboard + Search Engine Land confirmations + Glenn Gabe write-ups.

## Major updates with audit implications

### 2024

- **March 2024 Core Update + Spam Update (concurrent)**: Largest update of the era. Targeted scaled content abuse, expired-domain abuse, site reputation abuse. Many AI-generated content sites lost 50-100% of organic traffic. Audit implication: ON-024 (AI fluff) and ON-110 (spam signals) carry critical weight.

- **March 2024 - INP becomes a Core Web Vital**: Replaced FID. Audit implication: TECH-042 INP is now the responsiveness metric to flag.

- **May 2024 - AI Overviews launch in the US**: Citation visibility becomes a measurable surface. Audit implication: Team A5 + OFF-067/068 become more than informational.

- **August 2024 Core Update**: Recovery signal for some sites hit in March. Helpful Content Update folded into Core. Audit implication: helpful-content thinking applies to every core update from this point.

### 2025

- **November 2024 / January 2025 - Core Updates**: Continued tightening on E-E-A-T and originality. Site reputation abuse (parasite SEO) explicitly targeted. Audit implication: A1 must flag third-party content on subfolders if the main domain has different authority than the section.

- **March 2025 - Local pack tighten**: Citation consistency moved up in weight. Joy Hawkins and Andrew Shotland independently observed the bump. Audit implication: D2 NAP findings are now major-or-critical, not minor.

- **August 2025 - AI Overviews expand internationally**: Now in 100+ countries. GEO findings become globally relevant.

- **October 2025 - Spam Update**: Targeted exact-match-domain abuse, expired-domain abuse, and scaled AI content again. Many "second wave" AI content sites hit. Audit implication: AI-generated detection (TECH-080) sharpened.

### 2026

- **January 2026 - Helpful Content signals re-emphasized**: Google clarified that "people-first" content is judged on whether a user would feel satisfied. Audit implication: A1's helpful-content evaluation should weight user-satisfaction signals (dwell, return rate from GSC) where available.

- **March 2026 - Core Update + AI Overview ranking factor adjustment**: AI Overview citation patterns shifted to favor passage-citability over total domain authority. New sites cited more often when passages are well-structured.

## What does not change

- HTTPS, mobile-friendliness, page speed remain baseline requirements
- E-E-A-T remains the framework
- Local pack signals (GBP, reviews, citations, NAP, geo content) remain dominant for local

## How M3 uses this

M3 references this timeline when judging the strength of a finding's severity. "This site's traffic drop appears in the date of the March 2024 update; the audit finding ON-024 AI fluff aligns with the targeted abuse pattern. Severity critical is appropriate, not over-rated."
